Bench Press

Grip the bar shoulder 1.5x shoulder width apart and slowly go down then push the bar back up. Be sure to keep your shoulderblades tucked back and your wrists straight.

Cable Fly

Stand with one foot in front of the other. Set the cables to shoulder height. Tuck your shoulder blades back. Grab the two handles and pull them towards each other while keeping your arms straight, then slowly bring them back out.

Curls

There are many different curl variations you can do. One easy and effective one if Incline curls on a 75-45 degree bench. Be sure to focus on using your biceps and not momentum. To optimize lactic acid, synchronize your curls and don't alternate.

Squats

Adjust the barbell rack to be shoulder height. Slowly, go under the bar and place it on your traps. Pull your shoulder blades back. Grip the bar tightly and tuck your elbows down. Lift the bar off the rack, step back, and stand with your feet shoulder-width apart. Slowly descend and rise back up. Inhale on your way down and exhale on your way up.

Deadlift

Place the barbell on the ground and place your legs 1.5x shoulder width apart. Grab the bar slightly wider than your legs and push your legs like during a squat. Keep your back straight. Once the bar is above your knee, thrust your hips and lock your knees. Raise your upper body as well. Drop the bar then repeat.
