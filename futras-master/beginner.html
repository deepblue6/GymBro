Dips

Go down and then up

Pull ups

Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart. Bend your knees and lower your body as if you're sitting back into a chair. Keep your back straight and return to the starting position.

Face Pulls

Using a rope attachment on a cable machine, pull the rope towards your face while keeping your elbows high. Squeeze your shoulder blades together and return to the starting position.

Lateral Raises

Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart, holding dumbbells at your sides. Raise your arms out to the sides until they are level with your shoulders, then lower them back down.
